Product Case · AI · F&B

ALAIN — MAKING A MVP
AI-ASSIST DINING APP

Role Product Designer, Visual Designer
Duration May 2025 — June 2025
Company Cheese Aid Co., Ltd
Overview

Alain is an AI dining recommendation app for a food-tech startup. I led chat UX design, built style guide, and delivered high-fidelity prototype.

Contribution

Collaborated with the product team from brief to delivery, contributing UI decisions across brand, components, and screen design.

Context & Brief

AI generated image
Context of
Korean F&B Market

Running a restaurant in Korea has gotten harder — rising labor costs make it difficult to hire and train enough staff, leaving owners unable to fully deliver the experience they've built their recipes around.

Alain explored how an AI assistant could bridge that gap.

01 Analyzing

My first task involved mapping service flows for UI development.

During the initial kickoff, I interpreted project documentation from a UX perspective, providing critical insights that aligned the team with the user's needs.

02 Leading UI

Took ownership of the UI direction from wireframes to final screens, establishing consistent component patterns and interaction logic across the app.

Brand Strategy

Logo Mark

Logo making was focused on intuitive impression about F&B industry. Integrated with initial A and a cloche symbol symbolized AI-assistant F&B service.
